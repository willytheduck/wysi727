Crafting Happiness, One Slice at a Time

Pizza Heaven was founded in 2008 by Willy the duck, fueled by a passion for creating delicious pizzas made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. We believe pizza is more than just food; it's a way to bring people together and create lasting memories.

Our Commitment to Quality

We start with our slow-fermented dough, made with 00 Pizza Flour for a perfect balance of flavor and texture. Then, we top it off with a variety of fresh, local ingredients, including Peperoni, Cheese, etc. We are committed to using seasonal ingredients whenever possible to ensure the freshest and most flavorful pizzas.

A Warm and Inviting Atmosphere

Step into Pizza Heaven and experience a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our friendly staff is always happy to welcome you and make your dining experience enjoyable. Whether you're looking for a casual meal with friends or a special occasion dinner, we're the perfect spot.

We are proud to have been voted National Pizza of the Year.
